## 🎨 Design System & Visual Consistency

### Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: `#22c55e` (Agriculture theme)
- **Emerald**: Supporting farmers/growth
- **Blue**: Trust and connectivity
- **Purple, Orange, Pink, Teal**: Accent colors for variety

### Typography
- **Headings**: Plus Jakarta Sans (bold, tracked)
- **Body**: Inter (light weight)
- **Sizes**: Responsive scaling (mobile-first approach)

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover Effects**: Scale, glow, border color changes
- **Animations**: Staggered Framer Motion effects
- **Gradients**: Consistent use of gradient text and backgrounds
- **Glass Morphism**: Subtle transparency effects
